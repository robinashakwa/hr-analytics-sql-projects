# Compensation Analysis

## Business Question
HR wants to analyze employee compensation to ensure pay equity and manage workforce costs.

## Objectives
- Analyze average salary by department
- Identify high and low cost departments
- Compare salaries across genders
- Understand salary distribution

## Dataset
Employee compensation data including salary, department, gender, and job level.

## SQL Skills Used
- SELECT
- AVG
- GROUP BY
- CASE WHEN
- ORDER BY

## Key Insights
- Salary differs significantly across departments
- Some departments contribute disproportionately to total payroll
- Potential gender pay gaps may exist
